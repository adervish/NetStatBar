Roaming Detection

Know the moment your Mac switches access points

In a home or office with multiple access points, your Mac decides when to roam — and it doesn't always choose well. A roam during a video call causes a freeze. A roam during a VPN session drops the connection.

WiFi Scout shows you the exact timestamp and latency reading for every access point handoff, so you can correlate the roam with the problem and fix the root cause — whether that's repositioning a router, adjusting transmit power, or upgrading your network.

WiFi Scout

Privacy Policy

Your data never leaves your Mac. Full stop.

What data WiFi Scout collects

WiFi Scout reads network diagnostics from your Mac — things like signal strength, latency, packet loss, BSSID, and channel. All of this data is collected locally and stored in a database on your own computer.

Where your data goes

Nowhere. WiFi Scout has no servers, no analytics, no telemetry, and no cloud sync. Every byte of data the app collects stays on your machine, under your control, forever.

You can view it, export it, and delete it at any time from within the app.

What we would do with your data if we ever received it

Nothing. We have no interest in your network data, your location, your usage patterns, or anything else. Even in a hypothetical scenario where data somehow reached us, we would not look at it, analyze it, sell it, share it, or use it for any purpose whatsoever.

Third parties

WiFi Scout does not use any third-party SDKs, advertising frameworks, or analytics services. There is nothing in the app that phones home.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted here. We do not anticipate it changing, because the policy is "we collect nothing and do nothing with it," which is pretty stable.
